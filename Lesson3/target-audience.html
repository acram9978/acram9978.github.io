Target Audience

Description

The target audience is going to be people flying in or around Preston but can be used for anybody. Pilots will need to know how the weather is so that when they go up, they will know the conditions. It will also be helpful for students and other people on roadtrips.

Personas

  • Pilots
  • College Student

Scenarios

  • Pilot going up to fly around
  • College student driving to school
  • Someone going on a roadtrip
  • Kite Flyer
  • Family going to the park
